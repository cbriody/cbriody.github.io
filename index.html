About Us

Hi, We're Poppy and Emeline! Crocheting is more than just a hobby for us – it’s our passion, our creative outlet, and our way of bringing a little extra joy into the world.
We specialize in handmade crocheted stuffies, each crafted with love, care, and attention to detail. Whether it’s a neon narwhal, a cuddly cat, or a fun-filled fish, every creation is a reflection of our dedication to quality and creativity.
Crocheting has been a part of our lives for less than one year, and what started as a simple pastime has grown into a fulfilling journey. We love experimenting with new patterns, colors, and designs to create something special for every person.
Feel free to reach out if you have any questions, custom requests, or just want to say hello. We’d love to hear from you! Thanks for stopping by!

~ Poppy + Emeline
